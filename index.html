What Is the ClawBox Device?

The ClawBox device is a dedicated AI hardware appliance built from the ground up for one purpose: running your personal AI assistant privately, locally, and continuously. Unlike general-purpose computers repurposed for AI tasks, every component inside the ClawBox device has been selected specifically for AI inference workloads.

At its core sits the NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano 8GB — a system-on-module designed by NVIDIA for edge AI applications. The Jetson Orin Nano delivers 67 TOPS (trillion operations per second) of AI compute through its Ampere GPU architecture with 1024 CUDA cores and 32 Tensor cores. This isn't theoretical performance — it translates to real-world inference speeds of 10-15 tokens per second when running large language models locally on the ClawBox device.

Storage matters for AI devices. The ClawBox device includes a 512GB NVMe SSD that loads AI models in seconds rather than minutes. Large language models like Llama 3 (8B parameters) require fast sequential reads to deliver responsive conversations — the NVMe interface provides up to 3,500 MB/s, eliminating the storage bottleneck that plagues SD card-based solutions like Raspberry Pi setups.

The ClawBox device ships inside a custom carbon fiber enclosure with optimized thermal management. Active cooling ensures the Jetson Orin Nano maintains peak performance during sustained 24/7 operation without thermal throttling. The entire device draws just 15W — less than a desk lamp — making it practical to run continuously alongside your router.

Software is where the ClawBox device truly differentiates itself. Every unit ships with OpenClaw pre-installed and pre-configured. OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant framework that provides browser automation, multi-platform messaging (Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, Signal), email and calendar management, smart home integration, and extensible custom skills. Setup takes under 5 minutes: unbox, plug in power and ethernet, scan a QR code with your phone, and your AI assistant is live.

Why Choose a Dedicated AI Device?
